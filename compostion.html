    • Compositon,

    A side project focused more on the soundtrack aspect, rather than presentation of integration capabilities. Composition uses a visual trailer from Capcom's Resident Evil. Idea was to keep it simple, in order to provide a rather linear approach to not distract from visuals but to put viewer into this 'mesmeric' state and create tension. There are several factors within the structure.

    Most of this has been done in FLS, with both synthethizers and previously recorded audio. The sounds were processed and adjusted.
